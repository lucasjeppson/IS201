About us







At Vance Chiropractic, we help people recover from car accidents and everyday injuries through personalized chiropractic care. Our experienced team understands the unique challenges that come with auto injuries and works closely with you to develop a treatment plan that gets you back to feeling your best. Whether you're dealing with whiplash, back pain, headaches, or other injury-related symptoms, we're here to provide the compassionate, professional care you deserve.


We're not just here to adjust your spine. We're here to guide you through recovery, answer your questions, and help you understand what's happening with your body. Whether you're dealing with pain from a recent car accident or chronic discomfort that's been affecting your daily life, our approach is always patient-centered and focused on getting you back to feeling your best. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms, and we're committed to being your partner in health every step of the way.
